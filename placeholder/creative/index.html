Psychological Dot
Online Release Details

Psychological Dot is a blend of 12 and 18 month old golden wild ale aged in white wine barrels on to Vermont grown Purple Sage and our own wildflower honey for another 4 months. At the time of bottling in the summer of 2019 we zested Kaffir and Key Limes to deliver a mouthwatering vibrant minty, pinelike aroma with a wild juicy dry sour citrus finish. 5.8% abv.

Available for online presale at 8AM on Thursday, May 21st. Purchased bottles will be available for pickup starting Friday, May 22nd. Local home deliveries will begin Friday as well. There will be an option for those of you who do not live locally to purchase & reserve your bottles as well. $17/500ml bottle. Limit 2pp.
